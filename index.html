    How Microchips Save Lives

    A simple technology that saves lives

    1

    Permanent ID

    A rice-grain sized microchip is safely implanted under your pet's skin. It contains a unique ID number that lasts your pet's entire lifetime. No batteries, no maintenance needed.

    2

    Instant Scanning

    Any veterinarian, animal shelter, or rescue organization can scan the chip with a simple handheld device. The scan takes seconds and immediately displays your pet's unique ID number.

    3

    Database Lookup

    The ID number is searched in our registry database, which immediately provides your current contact information to the person who found your pet.

    4

    Happy Reunion

    You receive an immediate notification that your pet has been found. Within hours (sometimes minutes), you're reunited with your beloved companion.

    Why Microchips Beat Other ID Methods

    Collars and tags can fall off or become unreadable. Microchips are permanent.

    Collar & ID Tags

    • Can fall off or break
    • Text can fade or become unreadable
    • Can be removed by strangers
    • Limited space for information

    Pet Tattoos

    • Can fade over time
    • Painful procedure
    • Hard to read if fur grows over it
    • Registry databases often outdated

    Microchips

    • Permanent - lasts pet's lifetime
    • Cannot be lost or removed
    • Quick, painless implantation
    • Always readable when scanned
